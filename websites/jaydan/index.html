About the Game

Dragon Ball Z: Budokai Tenkaichi 3 is a fighting game that allows players to control various characters from the Dragon Ball universe. With an extensive roster and dynamic battles, it offers an immersive experience for fans and gamers alike.

Additional Information

This game features a variety of modes, including story mode, versus mode, and tournament mode, providing players with numerous ways to enjoy the gameplay.

About the Maker

The game was developed by Dimps in collaboration with Bandai Namco Games. It is known for its detailed graphics and engaging gameplay.
