Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas Information

San Andreas Wanted System

In Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas, the wanted system is a mechanic that allows the player to gain notoriety with the authorities. The system is represented by a series of stars, ranging from one to five.

  • The player has committed a minor offense, and the police will attempt to arrest them.
  • The player has committed a more serious offense, and the police will send a patrol car to pursue them.
  • The player has committed a major offense, and the police will send a SWAT team to apprehend them.
  • The player has committed a severe offense, and the police will deploy a helicopter to track them down.
  • The player has committed a heinous crime, and the police will send a full-scale task force to capture them.

Map System

The map system in Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas is a vital tool for navigating the game's vast open world. The map is divided into several regions, each with its own unique features and attractions.

The map allows players to:

  • Explore the city and its surroundings
  • Find hidden locations and secrets
  • Track down mission objectives and side quests
  • Plan their route and navigate the game world
