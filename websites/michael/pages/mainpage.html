*Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance* is set in a future where cyborg technology has become widespread...
In the game, Raiden faces off against powerful enemies, including Jetstream Sam, a master swordsman. The storyline explores themes of revenge, technology, and the struggle for control in a society driven by war.
**Jetstream Sam** is one of the most formidable opponents Raiden faces. As a former soldier turned mercenary, Sam challenges Raiden not just physically but also philosophically.
The gameplay of *Metal Gear Rising* is a departure from traditional stealth mechanics, focusing on fast-paced, over-the-top action, with Raiden's high-frequency blade being the highlight.
