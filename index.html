Museum De Pixel

Welcome to Museum De Pixel, a digital showcase celebrating iconic video game prototypes and projects. Explore creative works inspired by beloved titles like Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance, GTA: San Andreas, and Dragon Ball Z: Budokai Tenkaichi.

Experience the thrill of Metal Gear Rising's fast-paced action, the vast exploration of GTA: San Andreas, and the dynamic fighting mechanics of Dragon Ball Z. Join us in celebrating the magic of pixel art and the timeless gaming mechanics that inspire creativity!
