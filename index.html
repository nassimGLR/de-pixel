About Museum De Pixel

Museum De Pixel is an initiative by students to bring back the beauty of retro game design and showcase their prototypes. Our aim is to preserve the aesthetics of pixel art and classic gaming through creative projects that pay homage to the games of yesteryear.

Whether you're a fan of old-school platformers, pixel-perfect shooters, or retro RPGs, Museum De Pixel offers a unique experience, blending the charm of 80s and 90s video games with modern creativity.
